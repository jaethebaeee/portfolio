# Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement

**Dated as of:** [Date]

## Parties
This Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of the date first written above by and between:

**Assignor:** [Founder's Full Legal Name] ("Assignor")

**Assignee:** DoctorsFlow LLC, a Delaware limited liability company (the "Company")

## Recitals
WHEREAS, Assignor is a founder and/or employee of the Company;

WHEREAS, Assignor has developed, created, or conceived certain intellectual property in connection with the Company's business;

WHEREAS, the Company desires to acquire all right, title, and interest in such intellectual property;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual promises and covenants contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

## 1. Assignment

### 1.1 Intellectual Property Rights
Assignor hereby assigns, transfers, and conveys to the Company all right, title, and interest in and to any and all intellectual property that Assignor has developed, created, conceived, or reduced to practice, either alone or jointly with others, during the period of Assignor's involvement with the Company, including but not limited to:

- Software code, algorithms, and applications
- Patents, patent applications, and inventions
- Trademarks, service marks, and trade dress
- Copyrights and copyrightable works
- Trade secrets and confidential information
- Domain names and social media accounts
- All related goodwill and rights

### 1.2 Work Product
This assignment includes all work product, ideas, concepts, designs, drawings, specifications, and other materials created by Assignor, whether or not patentable, copyrightable, or otherwise protectable.

### 1.3 Future Rights
Assignor agrees to assign to the Company any intellectual property rights that may be developed or acquired by Assignor in the future that relate to the Company's business.

## 2. Moral Rights
Assignor hereby waives any and all moral rights, rights of attribution, rights of integrity, and any similar rights that Assignor may have in the intellectual property assigned hereunder.

## 3. Further Assurances

### 3.1 Cooperation
Assignor agrees to cooperate with the Company in securing, maintaining, and enforcing the intellectual property rights assigned hereunder, including but not limited to:
- Executing any documents necessary to perfect the Company's ownership
- Testifying in any legal proceedings
- Providing technical assistance and information

### 3.2 Power of Attorney
Assignor hereby grants to the Company a power of attorney to act on Assignor's behalf to execute and file any documents necessary to protect the intellectual property rights assigned hereunder.

## 4. Representations and Warranties

### 4.1 Ownership
Assignor represents and warrants that:
- Assignor is the sole owner of the intellectual property assigned hereunder
- The intellectual property does not infringe any third-party rights
- Assignor has not granted any licenses or rights to third parties
- The intellectual property is free from any liens or encumbrances

### 4.2 No Conflicts
Assignor represents and warrants that the assignment does not conflict with any prior agreements or obligations.

## 5. Confidentiality
Assignor agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all Company proprietary information and trade secrets, both during and after the term of Assignor's relationship with the Company.

## 6. Non-Compete
During Assignor's relationship with the Company and for a period of 2 years thereafter, Assignor shall not engage in any business that competes directly with the Company's healthcare workflow automation services.

## 7. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.

## 8. Entire Agreement
This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements.

## 9. Severability
If any provision of this Agreement is held invalid, the remainder shall remain in full force and effect.
