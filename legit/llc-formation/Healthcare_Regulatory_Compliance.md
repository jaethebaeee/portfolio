# Healthcare Regulatory Compliance Checklist

## HIPAA Compliance (Critical for Patient Data)

### HIPAA Security Rule
- [ ] **Business Associate Agreement (BAA)** with all vendors handling PHI
- [ ] **Risk Assessment** conducted annually
- [ ] **Security Policies** implemented (access controls, encryption, audit logs)
- [ ] **Incident Response Plan** for data breaches
- [ ] **HIPAA Security Officer** designated
- [ ] **Staff Training** on HIPAA requirements (annual)

### HIPAA Privacy Rule
- [ ] **Privacy Policies** and procedures documented
- [ ] **Notice of Privacy Practices** provided to patients
- [ ] **Patient Rights** documentation (access, amendment, accounting)
- [ ] **Minimum Necessary** rule implementation
- [ ] **Privacy Officer** designated

### HIPAA Breach Notification
- [ ] **Breach Response Plan** in place
- [ ] **OCR Reporting** procedures (within 60 days for breaches >500 records)
- [ ] **Patient Notification** templates
- [ ] **Media Notification** procedures for large breaches

## FDA Regulations (If Applicable)

### Software as Medical Device (SaMD)
- [ ] **FDA Classification** determination (Class I, II, or III)
- [ ] **510(k) Clearance** if required
- [ ] **Clinical Evaluation** for safety and effectiveness
- [ ] **Post-Market Surveillance** plan
- [ ] **Quality Management System** (ISO 13485)

### General Medical Device Requirements
- [ ] **Device Registration** with FDA
- [ ] **Establishment Registration**
- [ ] **Device Listing**
- [ ] **Unique Device Identifier (UDI)** implementation

## State Medical Board Regulations

### Telemedicine Regulations
- [ ] **State Licensure** for telemedicine services
- [ ] **Informed Consent** procedures for virtual care
- [ ] **Prescription Regulations** compliance
- [ ] **Cross-State Practice** rules

### Medical Practice Regulations
- [ ] **Corporate Practice of Medicine** prohibitions
- [ ] **Fee-Splitting** restrictions
- [ ] **Referral Regulations** (anti-kickback compliance)

## Data Privacy Regulations

### GDPR (If EU Patients)
- [ ] **Data Protection Officer** designated
- [ ] **Privacy Impact Assessment** conducted
- [ ] **Data Processing Agreements** with subprocessors
- [ ] **Subject Access Requests** procedures
- [ ] **Breach Notification** within 72 hours

### CCPA (California Privacy Law)
- [ ] **Privacy Notice** for California residents
- [ ] **Opt-Out Mechanisms** for data sales
- [ ] **Data Deletion** procedures
- [ ] **Privacy Policy** updates

## Cybersecurity Requirements

### General Cybersecurity
- [ ] **SOC 2 Type 2** audit (recommended for SaaS)
- [ ] **Penetration Testing** annually
- [ ] **Vulnerability Scanning** monthly
- [ ] **Multi-Factor Authentication** for all users
- [ ] **Encryption** at rest and in transit

### Healthcare-Specific Security
- [ ] **HITRUST Certification** (higher standard than SOC 2)
- [ ] **NIST Cybersecurity Framework** implementation
- [ ] **Endpoint Protection** on all devices
- [ ] **Network Segmentation** for patient data

## Insurance Requirements

### Required Coverage
- [ ] **Cyber Liability Insurance** ($2M-$5M minimum)
- [ ] **Professional Liability** (malpractice insurance)
- [ ] **General Liability** ($1M-$2M)
- [ ] **Directors & Officers (D&O)** insurance
- [ ] **Employment Practices Liability** (EPLI)

### Claims-Made vs Occurrence
- [ ] **Extended Reporting Periods** (tail coverage) for prior acts
- [ ] **Run-off Coverage** for discontinued operations

## Compliance Monitoring

### Internal Controls
- [ ] **Compliance Officer** position
- [ ] **Annual Audits** of compliance programs
- [ ] **Whistleblower Policy** and hotline
- [ ] **Document Retention** policies (7 years for HIPAA)
- [ ] **Regular Training** programs

### Third-Party Risk Management
- [ ] **Vendor Due Diligence** process
- [ ] **Contract Reviews** for compliance clauses
- [ ] **Subprocessor Audits** annually
- [ ] **Right to Audit** clauses in agreements

## VC Due Diligence Preparation

### Regulatory Documentation
- [ ] **Compliance Certificates** from auditors
- [ ] **Insurance Certificates** with current dates
- [ ] **Regulatory Filings** organized and accessible
- [ ] **Incident Reports** and resolution documentation
- [ ] **Training Records** for staff

### Risk Mitigation
- [ ] **Regulatory Risk Assessment** completed
- [ ] **Compliance Budget** in financial projections
- [ ] **Regulatory Roadmap** for scaling
- [ ] **Legal Opinions** on regulatory status

## Cost Estimates

### Compliance Costs (Annual)
- **HIPAA Compliance:** $50,000-$200,000 (personnel, audits, training)
- **Cybersecurity:** $100,000-$300,000 (tools, audits, insurance)
- **FDA Compliance:** $250,000-$1M+ (if applicable)
- **Insurance:** $20,000-$50,000
- **Legal/Consulting:** $50,000-$150,000

### One-Time Setup Costs
- **Initial Assessment:** $25,000-$75,000
- **Policy Development:** $15,000-$50,000
- **System Implementation:** $50,000-$200,000
- **Training Programs:** $10,000-$30,000

## Critical Notes

1. **HIPAA Non-Compliance Penalties:** Up to $50,000 per violation, $1.5M annually
2. **FDA Enforcement:** Warning letters, recalls, criminal penalties
3. **Insurance Impact:** Non-compliance affects coverage and premiums
4. **VC Due Diligence:** Regulators will scrutinize healthcare startups heavily

**Action Required:** Engage healthcare regulatory counsel immediately to assess your specific compliance needs based on your product features and target markets.
