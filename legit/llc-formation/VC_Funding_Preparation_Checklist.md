# VC Funding Preparation Checklist for DoctorsFlow LLC

## Phase 1: Legal Foundation (Complete First)

### Business Formation
- [ ] **Articles of Organization** filed with Delaware Secretary of State
- [ ] **LLC Operating Agreement** executed by all members
- [ ] **EIN** obtained from IRS
- [ ] **Registered Agent** appointed (consider using service like CT Corporation)
- [ ] **Business Bank Account** opened (separate from personal)

### Intellectual Property
- [ ] **Trademark** search and filing for "DoctorsFlow" (USPTO)
- [ ] **Copyright** registration for software code (optional but recommended)
- [ ] **Domain names** secured (doctorsflow.com, doctorsflow.io, etc.)
- [ ] **IP Assignment Agreements** from founders and early employees
- [ ] **NDA Templates** for investor discussions

### Cap Table Setup
- [ ] **Carta** or **AngelList** account created
- [ ] **Initial Cap Table** showing founder ownership
- [ ] **Option Pool** reserved (typically 10-20% for employees)
- [ ] **409A Valuation** obtained (required for employee options)

## Phase 2: Business Fundamentals

### Financials
- [ ] **3-Year Financial Projections** (P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow)
- [ ] **Current Financials** (if any revenue exists)
- [ ] **Burn Rate** calculation and runway analysis
- [ ] **Unit Economics** (CAC, LTV, Churn, Gross Margins)
- [ ] **Use of Funds** breakdown (how you'll spend VC money)
- [ ] **Break-even Analysis**

### Market & Product
- [ ] **Market Size Analysis** (TAM/SAM/SOM calculations)
- [ ] **Competitive Analysis** (direct and indirect competitors)
- [ ] **Product Roadmap** (next 12-18 months)
- [ ] **Go-to-Market Strategy** (customer acquisition plan)
- [ ] **Pilot/Testimonials** from early customers

### Team & Advisors
- [ ] **Founder Resumes** (LinkedIn optimized, highlight relevant experience)
- [ ] **Advisor Agreements** (scientific advisors for healthcare domain)
- [ ] **Board of Advisors** or **Board of Directors** formation
- [ ] **Key Employee Contracts** (with vesting schedules)

## Phase 3: Legal Documents for Fundraising

### Term Sheet Preparation
- [ ] **Term Sheet Template** ready (use NVCA or Fenwick & West templates)
- [ ] **Capitalization Table** scenarios (pre/post-money)
- [ ] **Pro Forma Cap Table** for different investment amounts

### Due Diligence Documents
- [ ] **Certificate of Good Standing** from Delaware (annual requirement)
- [ ] **Business Licenses** and **Permits** (if applicable)
- [ ] **Insurance Policies** (General Liability, Cyber Liability, D&O)
- [ ] **Lease Agreements** (if office space)
- [ ] **Vendor Contracts** (critical SaaS providers, payment processors)
- [ ] **Customer Contracts** (pilot agreements, LOIs)

### Employment & HR
- [ ] **Employee Handbook** (basic policies)
- [ ] **Equity Incentive Plan** (for future employee options)
- [ ] **Consultant Agreements** (for advisors and contractors)

## Phase 4: Pitch Preparation

### Pitch Deck
- [ ] **Problem/Solution** slides (market opportunity)
- [ ] **Product Demo** (live or video)
- [ ] **Market Size** and **Competition** analysis
- [ ] **Business Model** and **Unit Economics**
- [ ] **Go-to-Market** strategy and **Sales Pipeline**
- [ ] **Team** slide (founders and key hires)
- [ ] **Financial Projections** and **Ask**
- [ ] **Use of Funds** and **Milestones**

### Investor Targeting
- [ ] **Target Investor List** (50-100 VCs focused on healthcare/healthtech)
- [ ] **Warm Introductions** (network to get meetings)
- [ ] **Investor Updates** (monthly metrics for interested investors)

## Phase 5: Compliance & Security

### Data Security
- [ ] **HIPAA Compliance** (if handling patient data)
- [ ] **SOC 2 Type 1** audit (recommended for SaaS companies)
- [ ] **Privacy Policy** and **Terms of Service**
- [ ] **Data Processing Agreements** (GDPR compliance if EU users)

### Regulatory Compliance
- [ ] **Healthcare Regulations** research (if applicable)
- [ ] **Business Licenses** in operating jurisdictions
- [ ] **Employment Law** compliance (federal and state)

## Phase 6: Post-Funding Preparation

### Closing Preparation
- [ ] **Legal Counsel** engaged (Silicon Valley firm recommended)
- [ ] **Banking Setup** for investment receipt
- [ ] **Transfer Agent** selected (for stock certificates)
- [ ] **D&O Insurance** policy obtained

### Post-Closing
- [ ] **Board Meeting Materials** prepared
- [ ] **Investor Reporting** process established
- [ ] **Accounting System** upgrade (for audit preparation)

## Critical Timelines

### Pre-Seed Round ($500K-$2M)
- [ ] Complete all Phase 1 items
- [ ] Basic pitch deck and financials
- [ ] 3-6 months preparation time

### Seed Round ($2M-$5M)
- [ ] All Phase 1-3 items complete
- [ ] Product-market fit demonstrated
- [ ] 6-12 months preparation time

### Series A ($5M-$15M)
- [ ] All phases complete
- [ ] Revenue traction demonstrated
- [ ] 12+ months preparation time

## Cost Estimates

### Formation & Legal (One-time)
- Delaware LLC Formation: $500-$2,000
- Registered Agent (annual): $300-$500
- Legal Counsel (initial): $5,000-$15,000
- EIN: $0 (free)
- Trademark Filing: $250-$350

### Ongoing Compliance (Annual)
- Registered Agent: $300-$500
- Legal/Accounting: $10,000-$25,000
- Insurance: $2,000-$5,000
- Delaware Franchise Tax: $400 (minimum)

### VC Preparation (One-time)
- Legal for Fundraising: $25,000-$50,000
- Financial Modeling: $5,000-$15,000
- Pitch Deck Design: $2,000-$5,000
- Market Research: $5,000-$10,000

## Recommended Service Providers

### Legal

- **Cooley** or **Goodwin Procter** (healthcare focus)

### Accounting
- **Bench** or **Pilot** (startup-focused accounting)
- **Benchling** (life sciences accounting)

### Banking
- **Brex** or **Silicon Valley Bank** (startup banking)
- **Stripe Atlas** (incorporation + banking)

### Cap Table Management
- **Carta** (most comprehensive)
- **AngelList** (free for early stages)

### Insurance
- **At-Bay** or **Thimble** (startup insurance marketplace)

## Key Success Factors

1. **Network Access** - Warm introductions are crucial
2. **Product Traction** - Demo or early customers prove viability
3. **Team Strength** - Investors bet on people first
4. **Market Opportunity** - Healthcare automation is hot right now
5. **Timing** - Raise when you're ready, not when you need to

## Emergency Checklist (If You Need Money Fast)

- [ ] **Safe Notes** or **Y Combinator** application
- [ ] **Friends & Family** round (simple SAFE agreements)
- [ ] **Angel Investors** (accredited investors only)
- [ ] **Revenue-Based Financing** (alternatives to equity)
