# DoctorsFlow LLC Operating Agreement

**Dated as of:** [Date]

## Parties
This Operating Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of the date first written above by and among the Members listed on Exhibit A attached hereto (each a "Member" and collectively, the "Members").

## Recitals
WHEREAS, the Members desire to form a Delaware limited liability company under the Delaware Limited Liability Company Act (6 Del. C. ยง 18-101, et seq.) (the "Act");

WHEREAS, the Members have filed Articles of Organization with the Delaware Secretary of State;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, the Members agree as follows:

## Article I: Formation and Name

### Section 1.1 Formation
The Members hereby form a limited liability company pursuant to the Act under the name "DoctorsFlow LLC" (the "Company").

### Section 1.2 Name
The name of the Company shall be "DoctorsFlow LLC" or such other name as may be determined by the Managers.

### Section 1.3 Principal Office
The principal office of the Company shall be located at [Address] or such other location as may be determined by the Managers.

### Section 1.4 Registered Agent
The registered agent of the Company shall be [Registered Agent Name and Address].

## Article II: Purpose

### Section 2.1 Purpose
The purpose of the Company is to engage in any lawful act or activity for which limited liability companies may be formed under the Act, including but not limited to:
- Development and sale of healthcare workflow automation software
- Medical practice management solutions
- Healthcare technology consulting
- Any other lawful business activities

## Article III: Members

### Section 3.1 Members
The initial Members of the Company and their initial Capital Contributions and Percentage Interests are set forth on Exhibit A.

### Section 3.2 Admission of New Members
New Members may be admitted to the Company only upon the unanimous consent of the existing Members.

### Section 3.3 Withdrawal
No Member shall have the right to withdraw from the Company without the unanimous consent of the other Members.

## Article IV: Capital Contributions

### Section 4.1 Initial Capital Contributions
Each Member shall contribute to the Company the amount set forth opposite such Member's name on Exhibit A.

### Section 4.2 Additional Capital Contributions
Additional capital contributions may be required by the Managers for the operation of the Company. Such contributions shall be made in proportion to each Member's Percentage Interest unless otherwise agreed.

## Article V: Management

### Section 5.1 Managers
The Company shall be managed by one or more Managers appointed by the Members. The initial Managers shall be [Names of Initial Managers].

### Section 5.2 Powers of Managers
The Managers shall have full authority to manage the business and affairs of the Company, including but not limited to:
- Entering into contracts
- Hiring employees and contractors
- Opening bank accounts
- Making investment decisions
- Day-to-day operations

### Section 5.3 Major Decisions
The following decisions require the approval of a majority of the disinterested Members:
- Sale of Company assets exceeding $50,000
- Incurring debt exceeding $100,000
- Changes to the Operating Agreement
- Admission of new Members
- Dissolution of the Company

## Article VI: Distributions and Allocations

### Section 6.1 Distributions
Distributions shall be made to Members in proportion to their Percentage Interests, as determined by the Managers.

### Section 6.2 Tax Allocations
Profits and losses shall be allocated to Members in proportion to their Percentage Interests for tax purposes.

## Article VII: Intellectual Property

### Section 7.1 Ownership of IP
All intellectual property developed, created, or conceived by Members, employees, contractors, or agents in connection with the Company's business shall be the sole and exclusive property of the Company. Each Member agrees to assign and hereby assigns to the Company all right, title, and interest in any such intellectual property.

### Section 7.2 Confidentiality
Each Member agrees to maintain the confidentiality of the Company's proprietary information, trade secrets, and confidential business information. This obligation shall survive the termination of this Agreement.

### Section 7.3 Non-Compete
During the term of membership and for 2 years thereafter, no Member shall engage in any business that competes directly with the Company's healthcare workflow automation services within the geographic areas where the Company conducts business.

## Article VIII: Transfers of Interests

### Section 8.1 Restrictions on Transfer
No Member shall transfer all or any portion of their interest in the Company without the prior written consent of all other Members.

### Section 8.2 Permitted Transfers
Permitted transfers include transfers to family members or trusts for estate planning purposes, subject to the transferee agreeing to be bound by this Agreement.

### Section 8.3 Right of First Refusal
If a Member receives a bona fide offer to purchase their interest, they must first offer it to the other Members on the same terms before selling to a third party.

## Article VIII: Dissolution

### Section 8.1 Events of Dissolution
The Company shall dissolve upon:
- The unanimous agreement of the Members
- Bankruptcy or insolvency of the Company
- Expiration of the Company's term
- Entry of a decree of judicial dissolution

### Section 8.2 Liquidation
Upon dissolution, the Company's assets shall be distributed in the following order:
1. Payment of creditors
2. Return of capital contributions
3. Distribution of remaining assets in proportion to Percentage Interests

## Article IX: Indemnification

### Section 9.1 Indemnification
The Company shall indemnify and hold harmless each Member and Manager from any losses, claims, or liabilities arising from actions taken in good faith on behalf of the Company.

## Article X: Miscellaneous

### Section 10.1 Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.

### Section 10.2 Entire Agreement
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement among the Members and supersedes all prior agreements.

### Section 10.3 Amendments
This Agreement may be amended only by written consent of all Members.

### Section 10.4 Severability
If any provision of this Agreement is held invalid, the remainder shall remain in full force and effect.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the Members have executed this Operating Agreement as of the date first above written.
