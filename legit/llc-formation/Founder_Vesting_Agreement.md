# Founder Vesting Agreement

**Dated as of:** [Date]

## Parties
This Founder Vesting Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of the date first written above by and between:

**Company:** DoctorsFlow LLC, a Delaware limited liability company

**Founder:** [Founder's Full Legal Name] ("Founder")

## Recitals
WHEREAS, Founder is a co-founder of the Company and has been granted membership interests in the Company;

WHEREAS, the Company and its investors desire to ensure Founder's continued commitment to the Company;

WHEREAS, the parties wish to establish a vesting schedule for Founder's membership interests;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual promises and covenants contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

## 1. Vesting Schedule

### 1.1 Total Membership Interest
Founder has been granted [X]% membership interest in the Company (the "Total Interest").

### 1.2 Vesting Period
The Total Interest shall vest over a period of 4 years from the date hereof (the "Vesting Period"), subject to Founder's continued service with the Company.

### 1.3 Monthly Vesting
25% of the Total Interest shall vest at the end of Year 1, with the remaining 75% vesting in equal monthly installments over the next 36 months.

**Vesting Schedule:**
- **Year 1:** 25% vests immediately upon completion of 12 months of service
- **Months 13-48:** 1/48th (approximately 1.67%) vests each month

### 1.4 Acceleration Events
All unvested membership interests shall accelerate and become fully vested upon:
- Change of Control of the Company
- Termination without Cause
- Founder's death or disability

## 2. Forfeiture

### 2.1 Voluntary Termination
If Founder voluntarily terminates their relationship with the Company before the end of the Vesting Period, all unvested membership interests shall be forfeited.

### 2.2 Termination for Cause
If Founder is terminated for Cause (as defined below), all unvested membership interests shall be forfeited immediately.

**Definition of "Cause":**
- Gross misconduct or negligence
- Breach of fiduciary duty
- Commission of a felony
- Material breach of this Agreement
- Failure to perform duties for 30 consecutive days

### 2.3 Repurchase Right
Upon forfeiture, the Company shall have the right to repurchase the forfeited membership interests at fair market value, as determined by an independent valuation.

## 3. Transfer Restrictions

### 3.1 Lock-Up Period
Founder shall not transfer, sell, or encumber any membership interests (vested or unvested) without the prior written consent of the Company and its investors for a period of 2 years following an IPO or acquisition.

### 3.2 Right of First Refusal
If Founder wishes to transfer membership interests, the Company and other Members shall have a right of first refusal to purchase such interests.

## 4. Tax Considerations

### 4.1 Tax Treatment
The parties acknowledge that the vesting of membership interests may have tax implications under Section 83(b) of the Internal Revenue Code.

### 4.2 83(b) Election
Founder may elect to be taxed on the fair market value of the membership interests as of the grant date by filing an 83(b) election with the IRS within 30 days of the date hereof.

### 4.3 Tax Advice
Founder acknowledges that they have been advised to consult with a tax advisor regarding the tax consequences of this Agreement and any 83(b) election.

## 5. Confidentiality and Non-Compete

### 5.1 Confidentiality
Founder agrees to maintain the confidentiality of Company proprietary information during and after their relationship with the Company.

### 5.2 Non-Compete
During the term of service and for 2 years thereafter, Founder shall not engage in any competing business or solicit Company customers or employees.

## 6. Miscellaneous

### 6.1 Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.

### 6.2 Entire Agreement
This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties regarding the subject matter hereof.

### 6.3 Amendment
This Agreement may be amended only by written agreement of both parties.

### 6.4 Severability
If any provision is held invalid, the remainder shall remain in full force and effect.

### 6.5 Successors
This Agreement shall be binding upon and inure to the benefit of the parties and their successors and assigns.
