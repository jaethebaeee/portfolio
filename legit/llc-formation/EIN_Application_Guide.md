# EIN Application Guide for DoctorsFlow LLC

## What is an EIN?
An Employer Identification Number (EIN) is a 9-digit number assigned by the IRS to identify your business for tax purposes. It's like a Social Security number for your business.

## Why You Need an EIN
- **Tax Filing:** Required for federal tax returns
- **Banking:** Needed to open business bank accounts
- **Employees:** Required if you plan to hire employees
- **Contracts:** Many vendors and clients require an EIN
- **Licenses:** Some business licenses require an EIN
- **VC Funding:** Investors will require your EIN for due diligence

## How to Apply for an EIN (Free & Fast)

### Option 1: Online Application (Recommended)
1. **Visit:** https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online
2. **Requirements:**
   - Valid SSN or ITIN for the responsible party
   - Business formation documents (Articles of Organization)
   - Business address
   - Business description

### Option 2: Fax Application
1. **Download Form SS-4** from IRS website
2. **Complete the form** with the following information:
   - Legal business name: DoctorsFlow LLC
   - Trade name (DBA): DoctorsFlow
   - Mailing address
   - Business address (if different)
   - Tax classification: LLC
   - LLC tax classification: Check appropriate box (usually "S" election if single-member, or "C" for multi-member)
   - Business type: Professional, Scientific, and Technical Services (541511 - Computer Systems Design Services)
   - Reason for applying: Started new business
   - Principal business activity
   - Estimated number of employees
   - Fiscal year end
   - Contact information

3. **Fax to:** 1-859-669-5760
4. **Processing time:** 4-6 weeks

### Option 3: Mail Application
- Mail completed Form SS-4 to the appropriate IRS processing center
- Processing time: 4-6 weeks

## Tax Classification Options for LLC

### Single-Member LLC
- **Default:** Disregarded entity (treated as sole proprietorship)
- **Option:** Elect S-Corp status (Form 2553)
- **Option:** Elect C-Corp status (Form 8832)

### Multi-Member LLC
- **Default:** Partnership taxation
- **Option:** Elect S-Corp status (Form 2553) - requires all members to be individuals
- **Option:** Elect C-Corp status (Form 8832)

## Processing Time
- **Online:** Instant approval (get EIN immediately)
- **Fax/Mail:** 4-6 business days

## What to Do After Receiving EIN
1. **Update Business Records:** Add EIN to all business documents
2. **Open Bank Account:** Use EIN to open business bank account
3. **File Taxes:** Use EIN for all tax filings
4. **Business Licenses:** Include EIN on license applications
5. **Vendor Contracts:** Provide EIN to vendors requiring it

## State Tax ID Numbers
In addition to federal EIN, you may need:
- **Delaware:** State tax ID (filed automatically with LLC formation)
- **Other States:** If doing business in other states, get state tax IDs

## Important Notes
- **Cost:** Free from IRS
- **Validity:** EIN never expires
- **Changes:** File Form 8832 to change tax classification
- **Lost EIN:** Call IRS at 1-800-829-4933 to retrieve

## VC Funding Preparation
When preparing for VC funding, have your EIN ready as investors will:
- Run background checks
- Verify business legitimacy
- Check for any tax liens
- Confirm proper business structure
