## Veritasium-Inspired Scene Structure

### Standard 3-Act Structure
1. **Act 1: The Problem** (20%)
   - Hook with relatable scenario
   - Introduce misconception or mystery
   - Create curiosity gap

2. **Act 2: The Investigation** (60%)
   - Deep dive into science
   - Experiments and demonstrations
   - Expert insights and research
   - Build understanding progressively

3. **Act 3: The Solution** (20%)
   - Practical applications
   - Actionable takeaways
   - Future implications
   - Call to reflection
